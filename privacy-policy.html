Privacy Policy for Calyze

Last updated: December 2024

Calyze ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Calyze, we may collect the following information:

a. User Account Information

  • Email address and password, if you sign up manually
  • If you use Google login, we collect your Google account information (e.g., name and email)

b. Photo and Image Data

  • When you upload or capture food images through your camera or gallery, we use AI to analyze the food content
  • Photos are processed using Google's Gemini AI service for nutritional analysis
  • We do not use these photos for any purpose beyond analysis for your reports and food calendar unless you give us permission

c. Payment and Purchase Information

  • Calyze is a paid app available through the Apple App Store
  • We collect payment information through trusted payment processors (Apple App Store)
  • We do not store your credit card or billing details ourselves - these are handled securely by Apple
  • Purchase receipts and transaction data may be processed for app access verification

d. App Usage Data

  • Food entries, calorie tracking data, and nutrition information you input
  • App usage patterns and preferences to improve our service
  • Data is stored securely using Google Firebase services

e. Device Information

  • Camera and photo library access (only when you grant permission)
  • Device type and operating system for app functionality

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Verify your purchase and provide access to the app features
  • Provide core features of the app, such as food recognition, calorie tracking, and progress reports
  • Manage your account and log-in preferences
  • Generate personalized nutrition recommendations
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions (handled by Apple)
  • Communicate with you (e.g., for support or updates)
  • Improve and optimize our app and services

3. Data Security and Storage

Your data is stored securely and encrypted where applicable. We take standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

We use the following third-party services to provide our app functionality:

  • Google Firebase: For user authentication, data storage, and app infrastructure
  • Google Gemini AI: For food image analysis and nutritional information extraction
  • Apple App Store: For app distribution, payment processing, and purchase verification
  • Apple/Google: For user authentication and secure payment handling

These services have their own privacy policies and security measures.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share limited information with:

  • Service providers (e.g., cloud storage or analytics) to operate and improve the app
  • Authorities, if required by law (e.g., in response to a court order or legal request)

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access or update your personal data through the app settings
  • Delete your account and personal data at any time via the app or by contacting us
  • Request refunds according to Apple's refund policies
  • Opt-out of certain types of communications
  • Request a copy of your data

To exercise these rights or for purchase-related inquiries, contact us at calyzeapp@gmail.com.

6. Children's Privacy

Calyze is not intended for users under the age of 13 (or the age of digital consent in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without parental consent.

7. Data Retention

  • Account information is retained while your account is active
  • Purchase and payment verification data is retained as required for tax and legal compliance
  • You can delete your account and personal data at any time (purchase records may be retained for legal requirements)
  • Some usage analytics may be retained in anonymized form for app improvement

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this privacy policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date and notify users if required through the app or email.

10. Contact Us
